Dreamland

Time means that we can't do all that we want to and it takes a while to complete what we start. On the other hand our minds need not be tied to time the way our actions are.

Ideas (good and bad) are as easy to forget as they are to have. This section stores all the ideas and potential features for Replicante.

They may be awesome, needed/useful, meh, or even bad. The one thing they have in common is that it is not their time yet, and it may never come. But in case that time does come, there they are for us to remember.
